Speak now

        

 Speak Now is Taylor Swift's Third studio album, released on October 25, 2010. 
 It marked a significant point in her career, establishing her as a prominent figure in the country music scene. 
 
 Swift wrote all 14 tracks on the album by herself, making it a truly personal and independent project.
This is consistent with her songwriting style, where she draws inspiration from her own life experiences.

The album debuted at number one on the Billboard 200 chart, selling over one million copies in its first week.
It received critical acclaim for its songwriting and was a commercial success worldwide.

The album earned Swift several awards and nominations, including Grammy nominations. 
The song "Mean" won the Grammy Award for Best Country Song in 2012. Songs like Long Live and Enchanted
are fan favorites
