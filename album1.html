Taylor Swift

        

This album is Taylor's very first album released on October 24th 2006.
At just 16 years old, Taylor is the youngest solo writer on a debut country album.


The albums single, Tim Mcgraw became an instant hit and established Swift as a rising star
in the country music scene.

The album peaked at number 5 on the billboard 200 chart and spent a 157 weeks on the chart

Taylor Swift's debut earned her the honor of being the first country artist to receive the MTV Music
Award for the Best Female Video with Our Song in 2008
