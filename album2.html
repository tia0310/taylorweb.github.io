Fearless

        

 Fearless is Taylor Swift's second studio album, released on November 11, 2008. 
 It marked a significant point in her career, establishing her as a prominent figure in the country music scene. 
 

Fearless won the Grammy Award for Album of the Year in 2010. 
Taylor Swift became the youngest artist to win this prestigious award at the age of 20.

"Fearless" debuted at number one on the Billboard 200 chart and spent 11 non-consecutive weeks at the top.
It was the best-selling album of 2009 in the United States.

Taylor Swift is known for her songwriting skills, and "Fearless" features her trademark autobiographical lyrics.
The album includes hits like "Love Story," "You Belong with Me," and "Fifteen."

In 2021, Taylor Swift re-recorded and released a new version of "Fearless," titled "Fearless (Taylor's Version)."
